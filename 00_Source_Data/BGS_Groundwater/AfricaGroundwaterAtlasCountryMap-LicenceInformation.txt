These maps are called Africa Groundwater Atlas country hydrogeology maps (Version 1.0, 2019). 

They are digital maps in shapefile format, produced by the British Geological Survey as part of the UPGro Africa Groundwater Atlas project, 
in collaboration with hydrogeologists from across Africa. 

The Africa Groundwater Atlas is available at : https://www.bgs.ac.uk/africagroundwateratlas/index.cfm

More information on the Africa Groundwater Atlas country hydrogeology maps is available in this report:  
Ó Dochartaigh, B. 2019. User Guide: Africa Groundwater Atlas Country Hydrogeology Maps, Version 1.0. 
This report is provided as a pdf download with each of the maps. 
It can also be downloaded from the web here: http://nora.nerc.ac.uk/id/eprint/523272/
